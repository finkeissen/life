 # Meister Ekkehard

This project serves as a **reference projection**
of the `life` architecture.

It is not authoritative.
It is exemplary.

Its function is to demonstrate
how a concrete body of work
can carry core aspects
without resolving them.

---

## Projection scope

This project comprises:

- one novel
- one libretto
- a cycle of five operas

These elements are **structurally linked**,
but none is complete on its own.

Together, they form a **loss cycle**,
not a narrative arc.

---

## Core aspects explored

This project primarily explores:

- local coherence under global blindness
- responsibility without delegability
- irreversibility of action
- development under constraint
- failure as a structural phenomenon
- loss as an organizing principle
- resonance without moral discharge

---

## Emphases and distortions

This projection deliberately emphasizes:

- loss over gain
- constraint over possibility
- structure over psychology
- order as burden, not ideal
- clarity without power

It exaggerates:
- the visibility of misalignment
- the inevitability of escalation
- the exhaustion of moral relief

---

## Deliberate omissions

This project deliberately avoids:

- solutions
- recommendations
- progress narratives
- therapeutic interpretation
- pedagogical framing
- final synthesis

No form of redemption is permitted.

---

## Avatar structure

The project employs **avatars** as structural carriers.

Primary avatar:
- *Meister Ekkehard*  
  Carrier of order without power.

Secondary avatars carry:
- mediation without clarity
- action without order
- morality without responsibility
- regulation without reality
- belonging without truth

Avatars are not psychological characters.
They are positions within the architecture.

---

## Internal differentiation

### Novel

The novel functions as an **interior projection**.

It emphasizes:
- temporality
- exhaustion
- gradual loss of self-certainty
- development without improvement

The novel is structurally sufficient as a single instance.

---

### Libretto

The libretto functions as a **compression layer**.

It reduces the architecture to:
- voice
- decision
- scene
- irreversibility

It is not an independent work,
but a structural hinge.

---

### Opera cycle

Each opera represents one **irreversible loss**.

They do not form a progression,
but a sequence of structural cuts.

Indicative structure:

- Opera I: loss of naive order
- Opera II: loss of stable self
- Opera III: loss of delegability
- Opera IV: loss of moral relief
- Opera V: loss of excuse

The cycle ends without closure.

---

## Structural risks

This project risks:

- being read as allegory
- being mistaken for moral critique
- being reduced to pessimism
- being interpreted as instruction

These readings are structurally incorrect,
but anticipated.

---

## Evaluation

This project is not evaluated by:

- completeness
- success
- audience response
- coherence of narrative

It is evaluated solely by:

- fidelity to core constraints
- explicit self-positioning
- refusal of resolution
- structural honesty

---

## Status

The architecture of this project is **closed**.
Its realizations remain open.

All texts, music, and staging
are implementations,
not clarifications.

The project may remain partially unrealized
without loss of structural validity.
