# Life Phases (Structural Interpretation)

Life phases are not developmental achievements.
They are shifting protection structures.

## Phase 1 – External Order
Protection through authority, structure, dependency.

## Phase 2 – Separation
Loss of certainty, identity instability.

## Phase 3 – Illusion of Agency
Belief in shaping systems without full responsibility.

## Phase 4 – Burden of Responsibility
Non-delegability becomes visible.

## Phase 5 – Structural Exposure
No higher authority remains. No excuse survives.

These phases may overlap across characters.
They must not form a progress narrative.
