# Architecture – Ontology Definition

This document defines the ontological meaning of core terms used in the project.
These definitions constrain interpretation and prevent semantic drift.

## Order
Not harmony. Not morality.
Order is structural coherence that reduces entropy locally.
It does not imply justice.

## Responsibility
Non-delegable linkage between action and consequence.
Responsibility is not recognition, not praise, not guilt.
It exists independent of acknowledgment.

## System
A network of interacting procedures that persists beyond individual intention.
Systems stabilize through compensation, not truth.

## Power
Capacity to enforce consequences.
Power is independent from clarity or order.

## Morality
A relief mechanism that simplifies structural complexity.
Morality may accelerate collapse when misaligned with structure.

## Carrying
Sustained responsibility without external validation.
Not heroic. Not redemptive.

## Irreversibility
A state transition after which prior protection layers cannot be restored.
Irreversibility may be silent.

These definitions are binding for execution.
