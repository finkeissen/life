# Structural Energy Model

Without catharsis or resolution,
energy must derive from structural tension.

Energy sources:

- Increasing non-delegability
- Escalation through compensation
- Exposure of contradiction
- Silent irreversibility
- Body limits entering system dynamics

Energy must not derive from:
- Hope
- Revenge
- Triumph
- Revelation-as-salvation

Tension must emerge from inevitability,
not surprise.

If tension depends on withheld information,
architecture is insufficient.
