# Opera

This folder hosts **opera projects**
as embodied and collective projections
of the life architecture.

Opera emphasizes:
- simultaneity of voices
- conflict without synthesis
- embodiment through sound
- irreversibility of performed time
- mythic condensation

Typical strengths:
- collective resonance
- exposure of structural conflict
- non-discursive meaning

Typical blind spots:
- analytical clarity
- internal justification
- narrative precision

Structural constraint:
Each opera represents
one architectural cut.

No opera may claim
to represent the architecture as a whole.
