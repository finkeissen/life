# Glossary

- **Core**  
  The stable structural architecture underlying all projections.

- **Projection**  
  A bounded view that selects, excludes, and carries loss.

- **Layer**  
  A structural dimension of life, not a narrative sequence.

- **Misalignment**  
  Structural non-coincidence between perception, self-understanding, and reality.

- **Development**  
  Change in relative adequacy between capacity and constraint.

- **Responsibility**  
  Consequence-bearing commitment arising from bounded action.

- **Work**  
  A media instantiation (novel, opera, essay, etc.) derived from the core.

- **Export**  
  A compiled or published artifact; not canonical source material.
