# Structural Humility of the Architecture

This architecture is itself a reduction.

It sets forms.
It excludes alternatives.
It stabilizes distinctions.

It does not claim ultimate ontology.

It does not claim metaphysical finality.

It offers:

a working structural grammar
for human life
under constraint,
irreversibility,
and limited perception.

If future structural insight
demands revision,
the core may change.

Stability does not imply dogma.
